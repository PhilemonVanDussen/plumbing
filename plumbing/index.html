Long Lake Plumbing in Traverse City, Michigan was established in 2005 by Bob Walters, the third generation of master plumbers with the Walters name. Bob's grandfather, Herman, started the company, which John Walters, Bob's father, carried on as the second generation.

My Business Philosophy

At Long Lake Plumbing, doing quality work on time and at a reasonable price is what I'm all about.

I'll assess your needs, and let you know if I can complete the job in a reasonable amount of time. If necessary, I can also refer you to other specialists (such as electricians or masons) you might need to complete your renovation or home upgrade.
